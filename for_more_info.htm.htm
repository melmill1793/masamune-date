Date Masamune Historical Perspective

This museum is located in Matsushima, which has a reputation for being the most beautiful bay in the world. Nearby are other places famous for their connection with Masamune Date, like his family temple Zuiganji.

In addition to learning about Masamune, you can also try on samurai armor and paint a kokeshi doll (a doll made out of wood).

Date Masamune: The One-Eyed Dragon's Love and Ambition (1995 Film)

In this historical film you can learn about Masamune's early childhood and his evolution from wild trouble-maker to one of Japan's best warlords during the Sengoku/warring states era.
